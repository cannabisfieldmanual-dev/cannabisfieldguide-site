What to look for before you buy

  • Clear compliance info: A page that explains how products are lawful where shipped and the limits that apply. (If it’s hemp-derived, they should reference the Farm Bill and cannabinoid thresholds.)
  • Age gating: 21+ prompts or verification—no “anything goes” storefronts.
  • Real product photos & details: Strain names, batch notes, weights, ingredients, and plainly written effects.
  • Discreet shipping policy: How they pack, how they label, and what to expect at delivery.
  • Responsive support: A working email/chat and normal business hours. Bonus points for order-status updates.
  • Reasonable pricing: Deals are great, but if it’s too good to be true everywhere, trust your gut.

About that “Farm Bill letter”

Many reputable shops include a note citing the federal Farm Bill framework for compliant hemp-derived products. That letter is meant to explain what’s in the box and why it’s mailable. It’s not a magic shield: you still have to follow your local/state laws. Always check your own location’s rules on possession, shipping, and cannabinoids before ordering.
