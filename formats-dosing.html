Formats & Dosing

Start low, go slow. Everyone’s endocannabinoid system is unique. Use these charts and the calculator to plan a comfortable dose. 21+ only.

Safety first

  • Wait at least 2 hours after edibles before redosing.
  • Do not drive or operate machinery while impaired.
  • Store out of reach of children & pets.
  • Follow local laws and regulations.
