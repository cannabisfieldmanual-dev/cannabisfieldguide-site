Real-World Cannabis Dosing Chart

Start low, go slow. Everyone’s different. These are **adult 21+** guidelines—educational, not medical advice.

Smoking & Vaping (Flower)

Typical potency: 10–30% THC

  • Micro: 1–2 puffs — calm, clear
  • Low: 2–4 puffs — mood lift, social
  • Moderate: 4–8 puffs — strong euphoria
  • High: 8–12 puffs — heavy head/body
  • Expert: 12+ puffs — tolerance needed

Onset 1–5 min • Duration 1–3 hrs

Edibles (THC mg)

Wait **2+ hours** before redosing

  • Micro: 1–2.5 mg — very light
  • Low: 2.5–5 mg — beginner-friendly
  • Moderate: 5–10 mg — clear high
  • High: 10–20 mg — strong, long
  • Expert: 20–50 mg — experienced only

Onset 30–120 min • Duration 4–10 hrs

Tinctures / Oils (Sublingual THC mg)

Hold under tongue 30–60s

  • Micro: 1–2 mg — gentle ease
  • Low: 2–5 mg — noticeable calm
  • Moderate: 5–10 mg — balanced high
  • High: 10–20 mg — heavy relaxation
  • Expert: 20–40 mg — sedation likely

Onset 15–45 min • Duration 3–6 hrs

Concentrates / Dabs

Very potent; use low temp

  • Small: ~0.05–0.1 g (rice-grain) — intense, quick
  • Medium: ~0.1–0.2 g (pea) — powerful euphoria
  • Large: ~0.2–0.3 g — overwhelming for most

Onset 1–3 min • Duration 1–3 hrs

CBD & Ratios (THC:CBD)

  • 0:1 (CBD only) — non-intoxicating; calm, inflammation
  • 1:10 — clear-headed relief; daytime anxiety
  • 1:1 — balanced mind/body; pain & sleep
  • 5:1+ — THC-forward; strong symptom relief

CBD may soften THC’s intensity for some users.

Topicals (Creams, Balms)

  • Use: Pea-size on target area
  • Effects: Local relief; no “high” on intact skin
  • Reapply: Every 3–4 hrs as needed

Onset 5–20 min • Duration 2–6 hrs • Non-intoxicating
