Real Talk: dose light, stack slow

My favorite early strains: Blue Dream, Strawberry Cough, Maui Wowie, plus indica-leaning hybrids like Permanent Marker and Gorilla Glue (GG4). If you’re headed to an office or university, a vape is discreet and doesn’t cling to clothes. We stoners get noseblind—other people don’t. A quick body-spray/cologne pass and eye drops (if you get dry-eye) can save the day. Take it slow; coast through the whole day rather than nuking it with frat-boy doses.

AM dosing cheat sheet

  • Start smaller than you think: 1–3 short puffs. Wait 10–15 minutes.
  • Stack, don’t spike: Sip coffee/tea between pulls; assess before adding more.
  • Food first: Toast, yogurt or fruit keeps the ride smooth.
  • Hydrate: Coffee dehydrates; water keeps the “clean focus.”
